We own and remove the risk required to make projects investable.

Many infrastructure opportunities do not reach capital because key diligence gaps, incomplete derisking, and unresolved risk transfer prevent underwriting.

Site control, permits, interconnection, engineering scope, counterparties, and delivery risk need to be organized into a credible investment case.

Gude works at that stage. We identify the constraint, assemble the technical and commercial workstreams, and bring the project to a point where investors can underwrite it with confidence.
